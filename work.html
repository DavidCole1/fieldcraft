Lett's Property Care

A family-owned landscaping and interior cleaning business in Chesterfield, Virginia, working across ten towns around Richmond.

The challenge

Lett's does two things that usually take two separate businesses. Outdoor property work like mowing, landscaping, storm cleanup and hauling, plus interior cleaning that runs from deep cleans to commercial floors. One site had to sell both without feeling scattered.

On top of that, every quote meant a phone call and a site visit before anyone knew if the job was even a fit. And they were growing fast enough that hiring had turned into its own problem.

The approach

Split the services into two clear tracks under one identity. Then take the three biggest time drains, which were quoting, proving quality, and recruiting, and build each one into the site.

What was built

  • 4-step instant estimate calculator. Customers pick a service, describe the property, choose how often they need it, and get a real price range emailed to them. Built so the inquiries that come through are already qualified.
  • Before & after gallery. Real job photos doing the persuading instead of stock imagery. Storm cleanup, bathroom details, commercial floors.
  • Online hiring system. An application form with position and license questions, so recruiting runs through the site.
  • Reviews featured. Real reviews from Nextdoor and Yelp placed where they'll influence the decision.
  • Local SEO across ten towns. Chesterfield, Midlothian, Colonial Heights, Richmond, Chester, Bon Air, Brandermill, Woodlake, Swift Creek, and Moseley.
  • Trust signals up front. Licensing, insurance, and background checks stated plainly, because that's what a stranger needs to know before letting a crew into their house.
