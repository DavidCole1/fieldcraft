About

I'm David Cole. I build the websites myself.

Fieldcraft is my web studio in Alexandria, Virginia. I build sites for landscapers, cleaners, mechanics, and contractors. Every one of them is built and looked after by me.

First client: Lett's Property Care in Chesterfield. See what I built them.

Why this exists

Local businesses get a bad deal on websites. The agency quote lands at $10,000 and takes four months. The cheap option is a template that looks like ten thousand other templates. And the freelancer in between stopped answering emails around month three.

Meanwhile the work these businesses do is good. They're losing jobs to competitors who aren't better, only easier to find.

Fieldcraft is the option that should have been there already. A real custom site, built fast, priced for a business with a truck and a crew instead of a marketing department. That's why I started it.

How I work

  • You see it before you pay. Every project starts with a free sample built in your name. No deposit and no contract to look at it.
  • One person, the whole way. Whoever answers your first message is the same person who builds your site and picks up the phone a year later. It's me, on (202) 579-1580.
  • Fast, because I use good tools. No office and no overhead. I use modern tools to speed up first drafts, then go through every line by hand before launch.
  • You own everything. Domain, content, design, code. If you ever leave you take all of it, and I'll help you move.
  • I'll tell you when it's not worth it. If your current site is fine, or the money would do more somewhere else, I'll say so.

Straight answers

Questions people ask about working with a small studio

You're one person. What if you get busy?
I keep the number of builds low on purpose. If I can't start when you need me, you'll hear that on the first call along with a real date. I'd rather give you a date you don't love than miss one you do.
What happens if you're unavailable later?
You own your site outright and it runs on standard infrastructure any developer can pick up. Nothing proprietary, nothing locked. If you ever needed to hand it to someone else, it transfers cleanly.
Why should I trust a small studio over an agency?
Don't trust either on principle. Look at the work. The Lett's site is live right now, so click through it and try the estimate tool. Then let me build you a free sample and compare it to whatever else you've been quoted.
Do you work outside Alexandria?
Yes. The work is remote anyway, and Lett's is in Chesterfield, about two hours south of me. Being local is a bonus when it helps, not a requirement.
