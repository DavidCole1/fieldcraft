Common questions

Before you ask

Do I have to pay before I see anything?
No. You get a free one-page sample in your own name and branding first. You only pay if you want the full site built. If you don't like it, you owe nothing and we part on good terms.
I already have a website — is this still worth it?
Depends on whether it's bringing you work. If it's outdated, slow, broken on phones, or invisible on Google, a rebuild usually pays for itself. If it's fine and just needs upkeep, I'll put you straight on a Care Plan instead of selling you a rebuild you don't need.
Who owns the website when it's done?
You do — completely, once it's paid for. The domain, the content, the design, the code. If you ever want to leave, you take the whole site with you and I'll help hand it over. No hostage situations.
What if I don't have photos or a logo?
We work with what you have. Phone photos of real jobs beat stock photography every time, so even a handful helps enormously. If there's no logo, I'll set your name in type properly — plenty of good local brands are exactly that.
Can I cancel the Care Plan?
Any time, with 30 days' notice. You keep your website either way. It's month-to-month on purpose — if it isn't worth it to you, it shouldn't be hard to leave.
