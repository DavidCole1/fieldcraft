The Care Plan

Most websites die slowly.

A link breaks. A price changes. A phone update shifts the layout. Six months on, the site is costing you customers and nobody caught it, because it wasn't anybody's job to look.

That's what the Care Plan is for. Every month I go through the whole site, make your updates, and send a short report you can actually read. It's the difference between a website and a brochure quietly going stale.

Common questions

Before you ask

Do I have to pay before I see anything?
No. You get a free one-page sample in your own name and branding first, and you only pay if you want the full site. If it's not right, you owe me nothing.
I already have a website. Is this still worth it?
Depends whether it's bringing you work. If it's slow, dated, broken on phones, or nowhere on Google, a rebuild usually pays for itself. If it's fine and just needs looking after, I'll put you on a Care Plan instead of selling you a rebuild you don't need.
Who owns the website when it's done?
You do, completely, once it's paid for. Domain, content, design, code. If you ever want to move on, you take all of it and I'll help with the handover.
What if I don't have photos or a logo?
We work with what you've got. Phone photos of real jobs beat stock photography every time, so even a handful goes a long way. No logo is fine too. I'll set your name in type, which is all plenty of good local brands ever had.
Can I cancel the Care Plan?
Any time, with 30 days' notice, and you keep your website either way. It's month-to-month on purpose. If it stops being worth it, leaving shouldn't be a fight.
